Free-Spirited Faith = Having faith in God and the courage to follow your own intuition & personal revelation to guide you on your own journey living a Passionate life of adventure, growth & learning.

Mission Statement

The mission of Free-Spirited Faith is to assist souls in healing, passionately living their dreams, and accomplish their unique personal mission and purpose here on earth.

Our goal and purpose of our Healing from Heartbreak courses, live events and individual coaching packages is to facilitate healing, reignite passion, and facilitate moving forward with faith after divorce, betrayal, and heartbreak.

We also offer Passion Quest mentoring to help those who have simply lost their passion in life to reignite their passion, find their purpose and live a life they love, creating their dreams each day!
